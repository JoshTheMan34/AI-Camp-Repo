The Team

About us

We are a small team of 6 people that spent 3 weeks creating a fun and informative AI related website.

Week 1-2

For the first two weeks, Tyler, Joshua, and Hudson worked on data processing, while Drew, Vedant, and Jason created the model that would be used later on. The Data Processing team worked on finding usable data and processing it into a form the Ai could use. The Model Creation team's focus was creating the framework of the AI, and training it once they had the data from the other team. By the middle of week 2, we had already started creating the website and generating cards.

Week 3

Week 3 was all about website creation. At the beginning of the week, we split up into front-end and back-end teams to work more efficiently. The front-end team consisted of Vedant, Tyler, and Joshua, and they worked to make the presentation of the website visually appealing and made sure the website was user-friendly. Both teams worked in close conjunction to one another, and our teamwork made the project much easier.
